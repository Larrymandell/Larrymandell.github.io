About Pacific Genesis

Built on Experience.
Driven by Results.

Pacific Genesis is an international infrastructure advisory firm specializing in projects funded by multilateral development banks across Latin America, Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. Our principals bring decades of direct A/E/C sector experience — from project structuring and procurement to supervision and delivery — giving our clients an advisor who has sat on every side of the table.

We work with governments, private developers, and institutional lenders who need a trusted partner that understands both the technical demands of complex infrastructure and the institutional frameworks that govern how it gets financed and built.
